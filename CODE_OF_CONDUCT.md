# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance,
race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior

Use welcoming language, respect differing viewpoints, accept constructive
feedback, focus on what is best for the community, and show empathy toward
other contributors.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, discriminatory language, personal attacks, publishing private
information without permission, and sustained disruption are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report incidents privately to the repository owner through GitHub. Reports are
reviewed fairly and confidentially. Maintainers may remove comments, reject
contributions, or restrict participation when necessary.

This policy is adapted from Contributor Covenant 2.1:
https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct.html
