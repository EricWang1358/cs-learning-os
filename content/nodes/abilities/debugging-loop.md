# Debugging Loop

## Why It Matters

Debugging is an ability multiplier. It helps with algorithms, projects, tools, and exams.

## Core Idea

Reproduce the issue, shrink the case, form one hypothesis, test it, and record what changed.

## Common Confusions

- Guessing many causes at once slows the loop.
- Logs are useful only when they answer a specific question.

## Suggested Next

- Add minimal tests around the reproduced case.
- Track recurring mistakes as separate nodes.
